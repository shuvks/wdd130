Rivers

River Nexus is our fastest-flowing river only to be triumphed by the most cunning of rafters. With 1,209 yards, this river is an experience you do not want to miss! Attention: A life vest muist be worn at all times and rafters under the age of 18 must be accompanied with an adult.
River Nile is for the slow-and-steady rafters. This beautiful river is surrounded by beautiful greenery and occasionally wild-life such as deer. The river is 835 yards of calm and relaxing water, perfect for those seeking a more tranquil scenery.
River Kauma is for family and friends seeking to compete in various activities. Some of the activities offered are white water rafting, flyboard flying, spinnaker, and plenty more! This is 564-yard river is perfect for families and friends trying to make memories together!
